AJR

AJR is an American pop band founded by brothers Adam, Jack, and Ryan Met, collectively a trio of vocalists, multi-instrumentalists, and songwriters.

The brothers grew up in New York City, primarily focused on busking, singing covers and street tap dancing until shifting to songwriting and touring.
